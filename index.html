School Organizations

Inter School Science Competition
Inter School Science Competition

Our school once in a year under aryabhatta science center organizes a science exhibition among our school students in the first stage of this competition. The winners of this stage are sent to the next level of this competition i.e. district level competition.

Inter School Singing Competition
Inter School Singing Competition

Our School always once in a year organizes a singing competition among our students of Pragjyotika. The winner of this competition is awarded with the best singer award.

 Inter School Quiz Competition
Inter School Quiz Competition

Our School always once in a year organizes a inter school quiz competition among our student community. They make groups according to their respective class and in their class groups also they are further divided into some groups. the best three groups of the respective classes are awarded with a book and a merit certificate.
